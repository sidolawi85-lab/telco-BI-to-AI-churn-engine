# Contract Stability Engine

## Purpose
Stabilize Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) by converting high-risk Month-to-Month customers into longer-term contracts.

---

## Trigger Conditions
- Customer contract type = Month-to-Month
- Account active
- No downgrade request in last 30 days

---

## Segmentation Logic
Customers are tiered using spend and tenure:

- **Gold:** High spend + long tenure
- **Silver:** Moderate spend or tenure
- **Bronze:** Low spend + short tenure

---

## AI Actions
- Gemini AI generates personalized, non-aggressive offers:
  - Gold: price lock + premium support
  - Silver: discounted annual plan
  - Bronze: flexible hybrid commitment

---

## Automation Outputs
- Tailored contract upgrade email
- Offer validity window
- CRM tag update for follow-up tracking
