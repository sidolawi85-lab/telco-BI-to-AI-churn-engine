# Fiber Experience Sentinel

## Purpose
Prevent churn among high-ARPU Fiber Optic customers by identifying and resolving service quality issues before complaints arise.

---

## Trigger Conditions
- Service type = Fiber Optic
- Satisfaction score ≤ 3/10
- No open support ticket

---

## Segmentation Logic
- **New Fiber Users:** tenure ≤ 90 days
- **Tenured Fiber Users:** tenure > 90 days

---

## AI Actions
- Tenured users:
  - Gemini AI schedules proactive diagnostics
  - Personalized reassurance messaging
- New users:
  - Escalation to white-glove manual review
  - Priority onboarding intervention

---

## Automation Outputs
- Diagnostic task creation
- Proactive customer communication
- Risk status logged for churn monitoring

---

## Business Impact
- Reduces Customer Success workload by ~80%
- Protects high-ARPU revenue
- Improves perceived service reliability
